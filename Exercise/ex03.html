Stick Bug

This is me

Hello I am a stick bug. I may look like a stick but I'm actually a bug that mimics the apperance of a stick. I'm really good at blending in. I move with the wind and make friends with the tree branches. I wiggle and I climb and I stay still all the time.

I LOVE eating leaves!! I do not eat my fellow bugs, no way nuh uh!!! I prefer the tender crunch of green foliage between my mandibles...
